about us

Creative ideas are not always linked to big budgets, they are linked to passion, vision and finding out what exactly is the art of the possible. Credible If you try and imagine how many companies and individuals are involved in event management and production here in Kenya, you may think it would be hard to stand out. It is - but we do,VOLTZ Events House gives you inspirational ideas to stunning productions, strong themes and creative programmes, you can trust us to create memorable experiences that exceed your expectations. Regardless of our clients line of business, whether it’s a diplomatic mission, Blue Chip Company, corporate entity or private individuals, we’ll put in all it takes to make your event a memorable one. True to the word, ‘we love what we do’. But at the end of the day, what’s most important is that ‘you love what we do for you’. So whether it’s just one thing or you’d like us to take the whole event from your hands and market or brand it, rest assured, we’ll put our years of experience to task.
